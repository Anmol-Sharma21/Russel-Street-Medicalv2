WHO WE ARE

"Meet Our Healing Hands: Profiles of Our Expert Physician Duo"

Dr. Stephen Hill

Stephen Hill graduated from Auckland University in New Zealand in 2014 and obtained his Fellowship from the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners in 2017.

Over his training and practice, Stephen worked in internal medicine at the Royal Children's Hospital Melbourne before transitioning to General Practice.

Ms Kiyoko Tsu

Kiyoko Tsu completed her Bachelor of Nursing at the Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine in Singapore in 2019.

She is an accredited Nurse Immunizer and has worked in various hospitals within metropolitan Melbourne.

SERVICE AREA

"Connecting Care, Onsite and Online: Your Health Journey Begins Here"

Our medical center was known for offering a comprehensive range of medical services that catered to our community's well-being. From routine check-ups, vaccinations, and minor surgeries, to expert diagnosis and treatment of various illnesses, we were a trusted destination for quality healthcare. Our patient-centered approach ensured that each individual received personalized attention and care, promoting a healthier community overall.

In step with the dynamic healthcare landscape, our medical center has undergone a transformative evolution. We have expanded our services to embrace a more holistic concept of health, introducing specialized clinics for focused care, including advanced pain management, mental health and wellness, nutrition guidance, and chronic disease management. Our integration of telemedicine has furthered our accessibility, allowing patients to connect with our experienced professionals remotely. This progression underscores our commitment to providing modern, patient-centric healthcare that addresses the diverse needs of our community.
